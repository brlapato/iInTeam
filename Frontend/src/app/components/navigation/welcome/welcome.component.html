Remember your past
Keep track off all of your games and events from all of your teams. Keep your stats and see counts of your goals, wins and losses accross leagues. Keep game notes to remember cool plays or areas to work on. Your old games will always be available to go back, remember and learn from.
Organize your present
Know what games, practices and events are coming up, even when playing for multiple teams or leagues. Understand how your team is trending and figure out a game plan going into the next event.
Plan your future
Identify your goals and what you want to work on to develop yourself as a player. Keep track of what you are doing to work towards and achieve your goals. Take ownership of your development and achieve your potential.
